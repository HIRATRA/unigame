About

Unigame is a video game sales website developed by Hiratra

  • Up-to-date and original product
  • Ease of purchase and sale
  • Interaction between customer and seller, in addition to comments

Our platform is designed to provide gamers with a seamless and convenient experience, whether you're looking to expand your collection or make room for new favorites. With Unigame, you have the power to browse through a diverse selection of games, from classic titles to the latest releases, all from the comfort of your own home. Our user-friendly interface makes it easy to list your games for sale and connect with potential buyers, ensuring a smooth and hassle-free transaction process. Say goodbye to the limitations of traditional retail and embrace the freedom of Unigame, where gamers come together to explore, buy, and sell their passion for gaming.

Here is a manual that will help you better understand how our site works

It is important to emphasize that all your activities within the platform must adhere to community standards, or you risk facing restrictions.

  • 01 Create a UG account

    If you want to sell games on our platform, it's important to create a UG account so that the administrator can distinguish between the customer and the seller and assign them some privileges on the site.

  • 02 Subscribe now

    Access a range of exclusive tools and features that will allow you to:

    • Showcase your games and attract more potential buyers
    • Conduct secure transactions and avoid scams
    • Efficiently manage your sales and boost your business

  • 03 community standards

    It's necessary to adhere to the community standards and only sell games. Any product outside these conditions will be banned from the site
